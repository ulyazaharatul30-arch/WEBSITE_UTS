>> Synopsis

A group of high school students are trapped inside their school after a zombie virus spreads with terrifying speed. The outbreak began from a failed science experiment that turned humans into ferocious, bloodthirsty zombies and it's spreading fast.

With no outside help and no way to escape, students who were once worried about exams and crushes must now face unthinkable fear, the loss of friends, moral conflict, and betrayal in an increasingly chaotic environment. They must work together and sometimes against each other to find a way out before everyone they know is gone.

Beyond its gripping action and relentless tension, the drama beautifully explores themes of friendship, courage, sacrifice, and the will to survive. A Netflix smash hit that captivated audiences worldwide with its emotional depth and spectacular production.
