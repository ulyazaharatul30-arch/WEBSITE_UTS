>> Synopsis

Park Tae Jung is an ordinary man whose life is destroyed after being falsely accused of a brutal crime he never committed. Because of fabricated evidence and a perfectly orchestrated manipulation, Tae Jung is imprisoned and loses everything he has his freedom, his family, his future.

Behind it all is a mysterious figure named An Yo Han a ruthless and calculating manipulator who deliberately framed Tae Jung for his own dark purposes. After discovering the truth, Tae Jung begins a dangerous and action-packed journey of revenge, navigating a web of psychological games and betrayals.

With an intense thriller atmosphere, relentless action, and a chemistry between Ji Chang-wook and Doh Kyung-soo that is impossible to ignore, The Manipulated kept viewers on the edge of their seats right through its shocking finale.
