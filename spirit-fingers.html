Watch Now
>> Synopsis

Song Woo Yeon is an ordinary high school girl who feels invisible she blends into the background in a school full of louder, more confident people, and has never found anything she's truly good at. Then one day, she accidentally stumbles upon a group called "Spirit Fingers" a quirky, free-spirited art club filled with colourful people who create freely, without judgment or rules.

Through her time with the group, Woo Yeon begins to discover hidden talents she never knew she had, and slowly finds the courage to express herself for the first time. Along the way she develops feelings for the effortlessly charming and artistic Kang Ik June, who seems to see something in her that no one else does.

A heartwarming coming-of-age drama that beautifully portrays self-discovery, the power of art, friendship, and first love. Vibrant, warm, and deeply relatable Spirit Fingers is the perfect feel-good watch for anyone who ever felt like they didn't quite fit in.
