>> Synopsis

Ji-an has always relied on her uncle Jeong-min for everything he was the one constant source of love and protection throughout her difficult childhood. Then one day, her uncle is found dead under deeply mysterious circumstances, and Ji-an is suddenly thrust into a world she never knew existed.

As professional assassins close in on her from every direction, Ji-an must draw on the survival training her uncle secretly taught her over the years to stay alive. She soon uncovers the shocking truth: her uncle lived a double life as a weapons dealer who supplied hired killers worldwide through a secret online shop.

A relentless, edge-of-your-seat action thriller that masterfully weaves explosive set-pieces with genuine emotional depth and family bonds. Lee Dong-Wook delivers one of his finest performances, making this Disney+ hit absolutely impossible to stop watching.
