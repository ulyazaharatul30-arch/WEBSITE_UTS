>> Synopsis

Seo Mi Rae is a webtoon producer so consumed by work that she has almost completely given up on her romantic life. Exhausted by the complications of real relationships, she signs up for a service called "Boyfriend On Demand" a cutting-edge virtual reality dating simulation that lets users experience romance with perfectly tailored ideal partners.

Inside the VR world, Mi Rae meets men who seem to have walked straight out of her favourite dramas and webtoons. But the longer she stays immersed, the more the boundary between virtual and reality begins to dangerously blur and she starts to feel things she didn't expect to feel at all.

Meanwhile, back in the real world, her cold-on-the-surface but secretly warm co-worker Park Kyeong Nam keeps showing up at exactly the right moments. A charming blend of romance, comedy, fantasy, and modern technology that explores loneliness, escapism, and the messy beauty of real human connection.
