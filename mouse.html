>> Synopsis

This drama follows Jung Ba Reum, a warm and honest young police officer whose life is upended when Korea is shaken by a series of brutal murders committed by a mysterious psychopath.

When Ba Reum becomes directly involved in the chase for the killer, he uncovers dark secrets that begin to slowly change him from the inside. Together with hot-headed detective Go Moo Chi, they race to expose the identity of the killer behind a string of horrifying crimes.

The drama raises a chilling moral question: "What if a psychopathic gene could be detected while a baby is still in the womb?" Packed with jaw-dropping plot twists, psychological puzzles, and heart-pounding scenes, Mouse is one of the most unpredictable K-dramas ever made.
